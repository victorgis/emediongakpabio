About

Emediong Akpabio is a human rights and gender activist interested in child protection and development, women's rights, intersectional feminism, social justice, and policy reforms. He has been involved in the design of advocacy engagements across Africa and abroad. Emediong believes that we can create safe spaces for vulnerable groups if we have the right people doing the right politics, policies, and partnerships.
